Location: Wyoming. The park includes approximately 310,000 acres, or 485 square miles.

The park host approximately 3,885,230 guest a year.The highest number of recreation visits for one year in park history


The highest elevation of the park in 13,775 feet high. The second highest peak in Wyoming. The park extends from southern Alaska to northern New Mexico.

Grand Teton National Park was established in 1929, Jackson Hole National Monument was created in 1943. The two units were combined to become the present Grand Teton National Park in 1950

The park is home to a many wild-life species including grizzly and black bears,bison,moose,elk and pronghorns!

The annual preciptiation on average,Jackson Hole receives just over 21 inches of precipitation each year, half of that is snow during the winter months. During the summer its an average of 80s while in the winter 30s are what to expect!
